Same facts.
Your voice.

The news doesn't change. How you receive it can.

Choose your delivery style each morning. The facts are always intact — we just change the tone. Because sometimes you want the straight story, and sometimes the world has earned a little side-eye.

Plain
Just the facts. Clean, sourced, balanced. Nothing added, nothing removed.
"The Fed held rates steady at its May meeting, citing mixed signals on inflation."
Witty
A clever friend texting you the news. Light, warm, factually intact.
"The Fed did its best 'not it' impression again. Economists nodded along like they called it."
Snarky
Dry, world-weary, accurate. For when the news has earned the eye-roll.
"In a development that surprised no one, rates remain unchanged. The committee continues to exist."
Southern
Warm, folksy, colorful. Like a Charleston neighbor over sweet tea.
"Well bless their hearts up at the Fed — left things right where they were. Lord willing."
